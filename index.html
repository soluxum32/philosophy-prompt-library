Dueling Daemon

A philosophy prompt engine simulating a logical dialogue between two minds: the Socratic and the Nietzschean.

“Let contradiction breathe.”

About the Project

This repository is an ongoing exploration into how AI can simulate philosophical paradox by opposing two fundamental logics within one system. Inspired by the left and right brain hemispheres, it captures reason and fire in tension.
